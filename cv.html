Personal Statement

My progression from Developer through to Team Lead has given me the pleasure of managing teams of skilled individuals. Management came with challenges including conflict resolution and teaching either Agile or Lean software development practises.

As a Developer I enjoyed working with data, either from existing data sets or parsing it as it comes in. There are challenges inherent in both, but ultimately it came down to how fast the data could be parsed, and could the final product be maintained. I enjoyed pushing the boundaries of this "fast but maintainable" paradigm.

My career so far has primarily been in the financial services industry for this reason; the sector produces substantial amounts of data and needs quick responses.
